Test Instructions

Welcome to the Career Assessment Test. Before you begin, please read the following instructions carefully:

  1. Ensure you are in a quiet environment free from distractions.
  2. Answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability.
  3. There is no time limit,so take your time.
  4. Once you start the test, you cannot go back to previous questions.
  5. Upon completion, you will receive personalized career recommendations based on your responses.
  6. Consider all options before making your selection.
  7. Consult a career advisor if you need further assistance.
